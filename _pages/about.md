I am a PhD student at the University of British Columbia's Vancouver School of Economics. My research interests fall within labour economics, political economy, and public economics. 

I have worked at the Institute for Fiscal Studies, the Canadian Centre for Policy Alternatives, and the Bank of Canada. I hold an MPhil in Economics for the University of Oxford, a BA(Hons) in Economics from the University of Winnipeg, and a BMus in Piano Performance from the University of Manitoba.

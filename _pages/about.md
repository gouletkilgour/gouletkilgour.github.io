I am a first-year PhD student at the University of British Columbia's Vancouver School of Economics. My research interests fall within labour economics, political economy, and public economics. I have worked at the Institute for Fiscal Studies, the Canadian Centre for Policy Alternatives, and the Bank of Canada, and hold degrees from the University of Oxford, the University of Winnipeg, and the University of Manitoba.
